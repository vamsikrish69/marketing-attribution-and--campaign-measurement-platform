# Business Query Outputs

This folder contains screenshots of business-facing analytical outputs generated from the Marketing Attribution & Campaign Measurement Platform.

These analyses demonstrate how attribution models can be used to evaluate campaign performance, measure channel contribution, optimize budget allocation, and understand customer journey behavior.

---

## Included Business Analyses

### Top Campaigns by Linear ROAS

Identifies campaigns generating the highest return on advertising spend under the linear attribution model.

Business Question:

Which campaigns should receive additional budget allocation based on attributed performance?

---

### Channel Performance Comparison

Compares marketing channels across spend, clicks, attributed revenue, conversions, and attributed ROAS.

Business Question:

Which marketing channels generate the strongest performance?

---

### Attribution Model Comparison

Compares campaign performance under:

- First-Touch Attribution
- Last-Touch Attribution
- Linear Attribution

Business Question:

How do campaign outcomes change under different attribution methodologies?

---

### Campaign Disagreement Analysis

Identifies campaigns that rank differently across attribution models.

Business Question:

Which campaigns appear strong under one model but perform poorly under another?

---

### Channel Attribution Analysis

Measures channel-level contribution throughout the customer journey.

Business Question:

Which channels contribute most to conversion generation?

---

### Highest Contributing Channels

Ranks channels by total attributed revenue and conversions.

Business Question:

Which channels deserve the highest marketing investment?

---

### Customer Journey Complexity

Analyzes the number of touchpoints customers interact with before converting.

Business Question:

How complex are customer journeys before conversion?

---

## Business Value

These analyses help marketing teams:

- Optimize marketing budgets
- Improve channel allocation decisions
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Understand customer journeys
- Compare attribution methodologies
- Improve reporting accuracy
- Increase marketing ROI

The screenshots in this folder demonstrate the practical business outcomes produced by the platform.
